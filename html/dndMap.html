  DnD Map: Enchantia. When I started to plan my DnD Campaign I looked through a couple of world-building applications. The ones I found did not have a lot of features or elements that I wanted. So I decided I would build it in my favorite sandbox game, Minecraft. The first thing I did was draw the map and how I wanted it so that I could translate it into Minecraft. Once I had that drawn up I was able to start building it into Minecraft. I used a couple of mods to make the process better. One mod I use is World Edit, which helps with copy and pasting and making land more natural. I also use Journey Maps which allows me to see a map of my world from the top down view. This also helps me figure out where I want to place my cities and towns. The first thing I did was draw my map then I passed it over to Minecraft. I built a huge mountain in the middle of the world for the Dwarves to live in and a couple of rivers for travel and to split some of the cities. So far I have half of my cities built.
