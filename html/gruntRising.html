  Grunt Rising is a Belonging Outside Belonging game. A Belonging Outside Belonging game is a roleplaying game that tells the story of a marginalized group that creates their communities outside of a dominant culture. Games like this are Dream Askew and Dream Apart. In Grunt Rising you play as an underepresented race of creatures. These races are ones that usual DnD players don't choose.

Overview

  The heroes have won, the dark lords are dead. After centuries of hard fought battles, a promising new era of peace and prosperity smiles upon the world. Most of the world.
  After the fall of the dragons, the raid of dungeons, the massacre of monsters, the witch hunts, crusades, and castle sieges, those who would have normally made a living in the darker corners of this world no longer have a clear path.
  Some wish to integrate themselves with the new world, live in harmony, and move away from their old lives of pillaging and working for evil masters. Others wish to bring back the dark ages in a bigger, badder way, under a power greater than before.
  What will you do?
  Will you stand by your dark methods? Will you side with the heroes who vanquished the darkness? Or will you go on an adventure of your own making to do something else entirely?
  Wherever you go, and whatever you do, your purpose awaits.
