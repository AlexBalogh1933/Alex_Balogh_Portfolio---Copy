  Achievement Parkour Map. This map is based on all 110 (As of version 1.20) achievements in Minecraft Java Edition. There are about 100 different levels that show off the achievement. Some even will be awarded to you when you complete the level. Right now I am about 60% complete with building all the levels. Each level is hand-built to symbolize the achievements. Some of them are more literal while others hint at what achievement it is. At the time of adding this to my portfolio. Below are some of the pictures of levels.

Overview of the Map

Specifics on Different Levels

In this section I will go into some more details on some of the different levels that I have built and how they differ from other levels.

Getting Wood

To get this achievement you have to collect wood from punching a tree.

This level you parkour around some trees. You will notice one tree is a broken log signifying that the player collected this log.

Cover Me with Diamonds

To get this achievement you have to have any type of diamond armor in your inventory.

This level you are in a cave where you can see diamonds all around. There are also some crafting grides around showing the crafting recipes of each armor set.

Bring Home the Beacon

To get this achievement you have to power a beacon.

This level you parkour around on the construction of a powered beacon.

Hot Stuff

To get this achievement you have to fill a bucket with lava.

This level you have to parkour around a lava pool.

How Did We Get Here?

To get this achievement you have to have all 27 status effects at the same time.

This level has some of the ways people set up a machine to be able to get all the status effects.

Not Today, Thank you

To get this achievement you have to block any projectile with a shield.

This level you jump on the items to craft a shield. You can also see a player blocking a dispenser to simulate a shield blocking projectiles.

Sticky Situation

To get this achievement you have to slide down a honey block.

This level has something newer to parkour by using the honey to carry you to the next section of parkour.

Subspace Bubble

To get this achievement you have to use 2 nether portals to travel 7000 blocks.

This level has two 2D maps one of the Nether and one of the Overworld showing the distance between the Nether and Overworld.

Surge Protector

To get this achievement you have to save a village from a lightning strike.

This level has a Village house with a lightning rod on top. This level also summons lighting so that you get the achievement.

Caves and Cliffs

To get this achievement you have to fall from build height to the bottom of the world and live.

This level has you fall from build height to the bottom of the world then parkour to the next level. You get this achievement on the level.
